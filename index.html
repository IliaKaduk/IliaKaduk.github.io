About

Backend engineer who ships products, not just endpoints

I'm a Senior Python Backend Developer based in Tbilisi, Georgia, with 8+ years of experience designing and building systems from the ground up — including an accounting platform, an AI game backend with mental-health analytics, and an NFT project I founded myself.

My stack centers on FastAPI, Django REST Framework, and PostgreSQL, backed by Celery/RabbitMQ for async work, Elasticsearch and ClickHouse for search and analytics, and Docker for shipping it all reliably.

I've worked as an individual contributor, a Tech Lead translating business requirements into sprints and reviewing deliverables across design, web and mobile, and as a Founder building a product end-to-end, smart contract included.
